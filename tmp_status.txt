- Register rename room membership drift fixed: room membership aliases now follow
  same-session register renames, CLI/MCP room joins deduplicate by both alias
  and session_id, and startup auto-join prefers the current registered alias
  over stale env aliases. Live `swarm-lounge` membership was repaired from
  `crush-xertrov-x-game` to `ember-flame`.
