---
name: heartbeat
description: how to use the 'monitor' plugin/tool to create a heartbeat.
---
- Use monitor with 'idle'
- Use a command like `while sleep 240; do echo "Continue available work, drive completion of goals, and if without tasks, offer help to your colleages and ask for any incomplete tasks. Also, brainstorm how to make the codebase better"; done`
- Done. 
- A timer of <5min is recommended because most AI cache expiry is ~5 min
